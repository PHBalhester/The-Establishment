<objective>
Fix two randomness account leak paths in vrf-flow.ts and add a periodic sweep safety net in crank-runner.ts.

Purpose: Recovery paths create fresh randomness accounts but never close the original stale/orphaned account. Over time, this leaks ~0.008 SOL per recovery event. The periodic sweep catches any accounts that slip through inline closes (e.g., if the close TX fails silently).

Output: Both recovery paths in vrf-flow.ts close stale accounts inline before returning. crank-runner.ts runs a periodic sweep every 50 cycles.
</objective>
