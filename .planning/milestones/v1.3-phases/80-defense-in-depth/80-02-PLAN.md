Replace all truncating u128-to-u64 casts with checked conversions and add checked enum conversion for Token::from_u8.

Purpose: Prevent silent data truncation in on-chain math. While most casts are mathematically bounded by u64 inputs, defense-in-depth ensures any future regression surfaces immediately as an error rather than producing silently wrong results.

Output: All production `as u64` casts from u128 replaced with `u64::try_from()`, Token::from_u8_unchecked replaced with checked variant.
</objective>
