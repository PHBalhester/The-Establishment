1. `AuthorityBurned` — emitted on burn_authority

### Errors (9 total)
1. `NoWhitelistedParty` — neither source nor destination whitelisted
2. `ZeroAmountTransfer` — amount is 0
3. `Unauthorized` — signer is not authority
4. `AuthorityAlreadyBurned` — whitelist modification attempted post-burn
5. `AlreadyWhitelisted` — duplicate whitelist entry
6. `InvalidWhitelistPDA` — PDA derivation mismatch
7. `DirectInvocationNotAllowed` — hook invoked without transfer context
8. `InvalidMint` — mint owner is not Token-2022
9. `NotToken2022Mint` / `InvalidTransferHook` — mint validation errors
