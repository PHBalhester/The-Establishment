## Summary

H084 is a reference finding. The underlying issue has been analyzed in **H038** and is confirmed to be the same: fee percentage display defect in the legacy `computeQuote` path for sell swaps when smart routing is disabled.

## Quick Reference

- **H038 status**: PARTIALLY_FIXED (2026-03-21)
- **Affected code**: `useSwap.ts:448-449` (swapSolSell forward quote) and `useSwap.ts:521-522` (swapSolSell reverse quote)
- **Root cause**: Mixed-denomination fee summation in legacy direct-quote paths
- **Symptom**: For dust or near-zero inputs on sell swaps, fee percentage may be `"0%"` even when fees are present (but protected by guard against zero denominator)
- **Impact**: Display-only; execution and slippage protection unaffected
