# Finding: H128 - SellForm Correct Slippage on Net

## Status: NOT VULNERABLE
## Confidence Score: 10

## Rationale
At `app/components/launch/SellForm.tsx:177-178`, slippage is applied to `netSol` (after tax): `minimumSolOut = netSol * (10000n - slippageBps) / 10000n`. The on-chain sell handler at `programs/bonding_curve/src/instructions/sell.rs:184-191` computes `sol_net = sol_gross - tax` and then checks `require!(sol_net >= minimum_sol_out)`. The client applies slippage to `netSol` (gross - tax) and the on-chain check compares `sol_net` (also gross - tax) against `minimum_sol_out`. These are identical -- both use the post-tax amount. This is correct behavior: slippage protects the user's actual received amount (after tax), not the pre-tax gross.
