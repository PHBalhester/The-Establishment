# Finding: H081 - connect-src Missing CoinGecko/Binance

## Status: NOT VULNERABLE
## Confidence Score: 9

## Rationale
The CSP `connect-src` at `app/next.config.ts:19` does not include CoinGecko or Binance domains. This is correct by design -- the SOL price fetches happen server-side in the `/api/sol-price` route handler (line 44-76 of `app/app/api/sol-price/route.ts`), not from the browser. Server-side `fetch()` is not subject to CSP (CSP only governs browser-originated requests). The browser calls `/api/sol-price` (same origin = 'self'), which proxies to CoinGecko/Binance server-side.
