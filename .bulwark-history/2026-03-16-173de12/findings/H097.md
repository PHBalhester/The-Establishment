# Finding: H097 - Graduation Irreversibility Window

## Status: CONFIRMED
## Confidence Score: 8

## Rationale
The graduation script at `scripts/graduation/graduate.ts:320-366` executes `prepareTransition()` which calls the on-chain `prepare_transition` instruction to move both bonding curves from "Filled" to "Graduated" status. Line 351 explicitly warns: "IRREVERSIBLE: Transitioning both curves to Graduated". Between the moment graduation begins (curves locked) and the moment AMM pools are seeded with the withdrawn SOL + tokens (subsequent steps in the graduation flow), users cannot trade on the bonding curves (they're graduated and frozen) and cannot yet trade on the AMM pools (not yet created/seeded). This creates a dead window where no trading is possible for either token.

## Code Evidence
```typescript
// scripts/graduation/graduate.ts:351-362
console.log(`  ${RED}*** IRREVERSIBLE: Transitioning both curves to Graduated ***${RESET}`);
const sig = await programs.bondingCurve.methods
```

After this TX confirms, bonding curves are permanently locked. AMM pool creation and seeding are separate subsequent steps.

## Severity Estimate
MEDIUM -- Users holding tokens during graduation cannot sell until AMM pools are live. The window duration depends on how fast the operator completes the remaining graduation steps (could be seconds to minutes for a scripted flow, or longer if something fails mid-graduation). A failure during pool seeding would leave tokens untradeable indefinitely until manual intervention.
