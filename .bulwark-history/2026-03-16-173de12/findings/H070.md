# Finding: H070 - EPOCH_DURATION_SECONDS Hardcoded to Devnet (40s)

## Status: CONFIRMED
## Confidence Score: 9

## Rationale
At `app/lib/staking/rewards.ts:108`, `EPOCH_DURATION_SECONDS` is hardcoded to 40 (devnet: ~100 slots * 0.4s). The comment on line 106-107 acknowledges production will be ~30 minutes (4,500 slots). This constant feeds directly into `EPOCHS_PER_YEAR` (line 114), which is used by `calculateRewardRate()` to compute annualized APR. On mainnet with 30-minute epochs, using 40s would overstate APR by ~45x (1800s/40s). However, the function's JSDoc (line 134) and the `annualizedPct` field (line 95-96) are both marked as "DEAD CODE" -- APR/APY will never be displayed for legal reasons. The `solPricePerProfit` parameter is always undefined, so `annualizedPct` is always 0.
