# Finding: S005 - Staking + Crank Monitor Gap

## Status: CONFIRMED
## Confidence Score: 8

## Executive Summary

This strategy chains two confirmed findings -- staking escrow rent depletion (H017) and crank no circuit breaker (H019) -- into a cascading failure that halts staking rewards distribution and wastes crank SOL on futile retries. The chain works as follows: (1) the last staking reward claimer drains the escrow PDA below the rent-exempt minimum (~890,880 lamports), (2) the next epoch's `deposit_rewards` CPI from the Tax Program fails because the runtime rejects transfers that would leave the escrow sub-rent-exempt, (3) the crank runner, which monitors the Carnage SOL Vault but NOT the staking escrow, does not detect this failure, (4) the `advanceEpochWithVRF` call fails during the `deposit_rewards` step, (5) the crank's catch-all retry (H019) treats this as a transient error, sleeping 30s and retrying indefinitely, (6) each retry costs gas (~0.000005 SOL) and never succeeds because the underlying escrow depletion is not resolved.

The combined impact is dual: staking rewards are permanently blocked (71% of swap tax distribution), and the crank wallet slowly drains on failed retries. With no alerting (H004) and no circuit breaker (H019), this continues until manual intervention.

## Hypothesis Investigated

Escrow drained below rent-exempt (H017). Crank has no escrow monitoring. deposit_rewards CPI fails. Crank retries infinitely (H019) wasting SOL.

### Passive vs. Active Attack
This failure can occur passively (natural staking dynamics drain escrow) or actively (attacker stakes, waits for rewards, claims maximum to deliberately deplete escrow). The active version is faster and more targeted.

## Enabling Factors
- **No escrow monitoring in crank:** `crank-runner.ts` has zero references to escrow
- **No balance guard on claim:** `staking-builders.ts:366-375` -- no pre-claim escrow check
- **Blind retry:** `crank-runner.ts:308-314` -- 30s sleep, retry, no error analysis
- **No alerting:** Console.log only, no external notification
- **Thin escrow balance:** Escrow holds only deposited rewards minus claims -- can naturally reach zero

## Blocking Factors
- **Escrow receives ongoing deposits:** Tax distribution continuously deposits to escrow, replenishing it
- **Multiple stakers dilute claims:** With many stakers, no single claim drains the escrow fully
- **On-chain rent-exempt check:** The runtime prevents going below rent-exempt on transfers OUT, but does not prevent the balance from being exactly at or near rent-exempt after a claim

## Severity Assessment
- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Likelihood:** MEDIUM (requires escrow to be near-empty, which is more likely with few stakers or large concentrated stakes)
- **Impact:** Permanent halt of staking rewards distribution (71% of swap taxes), crank wallet slow drain on failed retries, protocol functionality degraded

### Combined Severity Justification
Individual findings are HIGH (H017) and HIGH (H019). The chain maintains HIGH because:
1. **Cascading failure:** Escrow depletion (data layer) causes CPI failure (execution layer) causes infinite retry (operational layer) -- three layers of failure from one root cause
2. **Self-reinforcing:** Failed deposit_rewards means escrow never replenishes, so the condition that causes the failure persists permanently
3. **Silent degradation:** No alerting means the team discovers the issue only when users report missing rewards
4. **Crank wallet drain:** Each failed retry costs gas, slowly depleting the operational wallet
5. **71% tax distribution blocked:** Staking rewards are the majority of tax distribution -- this breaks the core economic model
