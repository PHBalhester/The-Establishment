# Finding: H109 - Conversion Vault Whitelist Before Authority Burn

## Status: NOT VULNERABLE
## Confidence Score: 9

## Rationale
The hypothesis concerns whether the conversion vault's token accounts are whitelisted before the whitelist authority is burned. The initialization script (`scripts/deploy/initialize.ts`) follows a strict ordering: Step 9 (line 848-851) whitelists the 3 conversion vault token accounts (CRIME, FRAUD, PROFIT), while Step 25 (line 1652-1671) burns the whitelist authority. The script documents exactly 15 whitelist entries created before the burn, with conversion vault accounts explicitly listed at Step 9. The burn is the final whitelist operation (Step 25 of 25 main steps). The idempotency checks also ensure that re-runs skip already-whitelisted accounts and already-burned authority. The ordering is correct and safe.
