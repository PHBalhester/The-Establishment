# S002: Bounty Deadlock Does NOT Enable Tax Rate Freezing Attack

**Strategy:** S002
**Determination:** NOT VULNERABLE
**Severity:** N/A
**Confidence:** HIGH

## Summary

This investigation examined whether an attacker could exploit the H001 bounty rent-exempt bug to freeze tax rates at a favorable (low) value indefinitely. The hypothesis was that draining the bounty vault to trigger epoch transition deadlock would preserve the current tax regime permanently, benefiting high-volume traders who timed the attack during favorable rate periods.

**Determination:** The attack is **NOT VIABLE** because tax rates persist across epoch freeze, but the vault can be manually replenished without admin action by ANY user via normal trading activity. The protocol's 24% carnage tax allocation continuously refills the vault during normal operations, creating a self-healing mechanism that defeats the intended deadlock exploitation.

## Evidence

### Tax Rate Persistence (CONFIRMED)

**Location:** `/Users/mlbob/Projects/Dr Fraudsworth/programs/tax-program/src/state/epoch_state_reader.rs:68-74`

Tax rates are read from the EpochState account on EVERY swap:

```rust
pub fn get_tax_bps(&self, is_crime: bool, is_buy: bool) -> u16 {
    match (is_crime, is_buy) {
        (true, true) => self.crime_buy_tax_bps,
        (true, false) => self.crime_sell_tax_bps,
        (false, true) => self.fraud_buy_tax_bps,
        (false, false) => self.fraud_sell_tax_bps,
    }
}
```

**Implication:** If epochs freeze (cannot call `consume_randomness` to update rates), the tax rates remain at their last-set values. This confirms the first half of the attack hypothesis.

### Tax Rate Update Mechanism

**Location:** `/Users/mlbob/Projects/Dr Fraudsworth/programs/epoch-program/src/instructions/consume_randomness.rs:192-207`

Tax rates are updated during `consume_randomness`:


**Implication:** Without epoch transitions (which require successful `trigger_epoch_transition`), tax rates cannot update. This confirms the second half of the attack hypothesis.

### Vault Replenishment Mechanism (DEFEATS ATTACK)

**Location:** `/Users/mlbob/Projects/Dr Fraudsworth/programs/tax-program/src/instructions/swap_sol_buy.rs` (and `swap_sol_sell.rs`)

The carnage vault is funded by **24% of all trading tax** (per ECON-02 context file, line 41):

```
Tax split: 75% staking / 24% carnage / 1% treasury
```

Every buy/sell swap executes:
1. Calculate tax (1-14% of swap amount)
2. Split tax: `carnage_portion = floor(tax * 24 / 100)`
3. Transfer carnage portion to `carnage_sol_vault` PDA

**Critical Finding:** The vault replenishment happens BEFORE the epoch transition check. Trading continues normally even if epoch transitions are frozen. As long as ANY trading occurs, the carnage vault accumulates SOL.

### Vault Balance Threshold for Recovery

From H001 finding:
- Bounty amount: 1,000,000 lamports (0.001 SOL)
- Rent-exempt minimum: 890,880 lamports
- **Minimum vault balance to unblock**: 1,890,880 lamports (0.00189088 SOL)

**Recovery calculation:**
- If vault is at 0 lamports (worst case)
- Tax rate: 14% (maximum sell tax, worst case for volume)
- 24% of tax goes to carnage vault
- Net to vault: `14% * 24% = 3.36%` of swap amount

**To accumulate 1,890,880 lamports:**
- Required swap volume: `1,890,880 / 0.0336 = 56,264,286 lamports = 0.056 SOL`

**In USD terms** (at $100/SOL): `$5.60` in trading volume recovers the vault.

### Permissionless Vault Funding

**Critical observation:** The carnage vault PDA has NO access control on incoming transfers. Any user (including the attacker themselves, honest users, or MEV bots) can:

1. Execute swaps during the "frozen" epoch
2. Automatically contribute 24% of their tax to the vault
3. Bring vault balance above the recovery threshold
4. Enable the next epoch transition to succeed

**No admin intervention required.** The vault refills through normal protocol operations.

## Attack Scenario Analysis

### Attack Flow (as hypothesized)

1. **T0**: VRF sets favorable tax rates (e.g., CRIME buy = 1%, sell = 14%)
2. **T1**: Attacker observes vault balance in danger zone [1M, 1.89M lamports]
3. **T2**: Attacker triggers epoch transition → fails due to H001 rent bug
4. **T3**: Epochs are frozen, tax rates cannot update
5. **T4**: Attacker expects to trade indefinitely at favorable 1% buy tax

### Why Attack Fails

**Between T3 and T4:**
- Attacker's own trades (to exploit low tax) refill the vault
- Every 0.056 SOL traded adds enough to vault to unblock
- Other users' trades also contribute
- MEV bots performing arbitrage contribute

**Numerical example:**
- Attacker wants to buy 100 SOL worth of CRIME at 1% tax
- Tax collected: 1 SOL
- Carnage portion: 0.24 SOL = 240,000,000 lamports
- **This is 127x the recovery threshold**

**Result:** By the time the attacker executes even a single medium-sized trade to "exploit" the frozen low tax, they have already contributed enough to the vault to unblock epoch transitions. The next user (or a crank service) can immediately call `trigger_epoch_transition` successfully, unfreezing the epoch and allowing rates to update.

### Economic Irrationality

**Cost-benefit for attacker:**
1. **Setup cost**: Monitor vault balance, time the drain attack
2. **Benefit window**: 0 blocks (vault refills on first trade)
3. **Exposure**: The attacker must trade DURING the frozen period to gain any benefit, but trading IS the recovery mechanism
4. **Net gain**: Negative (gas costs to trigger deadlock, zero duration of exploitation)

**Self-defeating attack:** The very action the attacker wants to perform (trading at low tax) is the action that defeats the attack (refilling the vault).

## Vault Depletion Rate vs Refill Rate

### Depletion Sources
- Epoch trigger bounty: 1M lamports per epoch (devnet: every 750 slots ≈ 5 min, mainnet: every 4500 slots ≈ 30 min)
- Carnage execution: up to 1000 SOL per carnage event (~4.3% trigger probability per epoch)

### Refill Sources
- Trading tax: 24% of all taxes
- Continuous (every swap)

**At equilibrium:**
- Daily trading volume (conservative): $10,000 (from ECON context)
- Average tax: 7.5%
- Daily tax: $750
- Daily carnage vault: $180 (24% of tax)
- **Daily vault accumulation: ~180,000,000 lamports**

**Bounty drain:**
- Mainnet: ~48 epochs/day * 1M lamports = 48M lamports/day
- **Net daily surplus: +132M lamports**

**Conclusion:** Under normal trading conditions, the vault accumulates faster than bounties drain it. The H001 bug only manifests during extended low-liquidity periods, not during active trading (which is when an attacker would want to exploit frozen tax rates).

## Cross-Reference to H001

**H001 remains a CRITICAL bug** for different reasons:
- **Organic deadlock risk**: During extended low-volume periods (e.g., bear market, protocol maturity), the vault can deplete naturally
- **Protocol liveness**: Frozen epochs prevent carnage execution, staking reward distribution, and VRF randomness consumption
- **User experience**: Indefinite freeze damages protocol reputation even if trading continues

**H001 is NOT exploitable for tax rate manipulation** because:
- Vault refills passively through trading
- No admin action required to recover
- Attack window is self-defeating (trading both exploits low rates AND refills vault)
