## What Is This, Exactly?

Dr. Fraudsworth's Fantastical Finance Factory is a three-token protocol on Solana where:

- **Trading generates fees.** Every swap through the CRIME and FRAUD pools incur a variable tax taken in SOL (1-4% on the cheap side, 11-14% on the expensive side).
- **Fees fund rewards.** 71% of all taxes go to PROFIT stakers as rewards. 24% feeds the Carnage Fund. 5% to the treasury.
- **Randomness creates opportunity.** Each epoch, a verifiable random number from Switchboard VRF reshuffles the tax rates, creating windows for arbitrage traders to compete.
- **Deflation adds chaos.** A Carnage Fund randomly triggers roughly twice per day, market-buying and burning tokens to keep things interesting.

The result is a closed-loop economy where traders and reward-seekers need each other. Neither side can profit without the other. The Doctor calls this "symbiotic finance." His lawyer calls it "surprisingly legal."

## The Vision: A Finance Factory

Think of the protocol as a factory with three production lines.

1. **Tax asymmetry** creates raw material (price dislocations between CRIME and FRAUD).
2. **Arbitrage** converts that raw material into rewards (SOL taxes from trading volume).
3. **Reward distribution** pays the workers (PROFIT stakers who provide liquidity depth).

The Carnage Fund is the factory's quality control. Periodically pulling tokens off the line, deciding they aren't good enough and throwing them into the incinerator. This reduces supply, causes Carnage and creates more raw material for the next cycle.

The factory runs 24/7. No holidays. No maintenance windows. No human operators required.

## What Makes This Different

### Real Rewards, Not Emissions

Most DeFi protocols print tokens and hand them out as "rewards". The token price drops, your rewards evaporate and the only winners are the people who got in early and got out fast.

Here, rewards come from one source: SOL collected as trading taxes. No new tokens are ever minted after launch. The supply of CRIME and FRAUD only goes *down* over time. PROFIT supply is fixed at 20 million, but the possible circulating supply decreases as the outer tokens get burnt over time.

### No Ponzinomics

The protocol does not need new money to reward existing holders. Rewards are funded by trading friction. If nobody trades, nobody earns. If trading volume is high, everyone earns. Simple cause and effect.

### Verifiable Randomness

Tax regimes are not decided by an admin or a gut feeling. They are determined by Switchboard VRF, **verifiable random functions running inside TEE-secured oracles that produce provably fair results on-chain.**

### Fair Launch

Two identical bonding curves handle initial CRIME and FRAUD distribution. No presale, no VC allocation, no team tokens. Both curves must complete for the protocol to go live, if either fails then all participants can burn their tokens in return for a proportionate share of the SOL left in the curves.

PROFIT cannot be purchased directly. It is acquired by converting CRIME or FRAUD through the conversion vault at a fixed 100:1 rate.

## Built on Solana

The entire protocol runs on Solana:

- **Seven on-chain programs** (AMM, Tax, Epoch/VRF, Staking, Transfer Hook, Conversion Vault, Bonding Curve) -- all written in Rust with the Anchor framework
- **Token-2022** with transfer hooks enforcing whitelist-based transfer validation on all three tokens (CRIME, FRAUD, and PROFIT)
- **Switchboard VRF** for provably fair randomness every epoch
- **Sub-second finality** so arbitrage traders can compete in real time
- **Low transaction costs** so claiming rewards does not eat into your returns
