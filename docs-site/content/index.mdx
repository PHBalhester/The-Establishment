## How Does It Work?

Every epoch, the protocol rolls new tax rates using Switchboard VRF. One token becomes cheap to buy, the other becomes expensive. This asymmetry creates arbitrage opportunities designed to generate real, consistent trading volume.

That volume is taxable. 71% goes directly to PROFIT stakers, paid out in SOL rewards. The remaining 24% feeds the Carnage Fund, and 5% goes to the treasury.

The **Carnage Fund** accumulates its share and randomly detonates. Market-buying tokens and burning them permanently. Deflation, chaos, massive de-pegs, all in one package.

## What Makes This Different?

**Real rewards, not printed tokens.** Every reward comes directly from genuine trading activity, not from inflating supply or promising unsustainable returns. No volume? No rewards. The 71/24/5 split is hardcoded and enforced into every swap.

**Provably fair randomness.** Tax regimes are determined by Switchboard VRF with TEE-secured oracles. Nobody, not even the Doctor, can predict or manipulate the next epoch's rates.

**Seven on-chain programs.** AMM, Tax, Epoch/VRF, Staking, Transfer Hooks, Conversion Vault and Bonding Curve. All written in Rust with Anchor, every program is deployed on Solana and verifiable by anyone.

**Deflationary by design.** The Carnage Fund permanently burns CRIME and FRAUD tokens, reducing supply over time. No new tokens can ever be minted, supply only gets tighter.

**Fair launch via bonding curves.** No presale, no VC allocation, no insider tokens.
Two identical linear bonding curves handle initial distribution with full price transparency.
