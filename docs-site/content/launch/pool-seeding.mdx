# Pool Seeding

Once both bonding curves fill their 500 SOL targets, the protocol transitions from fundraising to live trading. This transition --- pool seeding --- creates the liquidity pools, seeds the conversion vault, and activates the epoch system.

The transition is **permissionless**. Anyone can trigger `finalize_transition` once both curves reach `Filled` status. No admin approval. No multisig ceremony. The code checks the conditions and either proceeds or reverts.

---

## The Two SOL Pools

Two constant-product AMM pools are created with protocol-owned liquidity:

| Pool | Token Side | Amount | SOL Side | Amount | LP Fee |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|--------|--------|
| CRIME/SOL | CRIME | 290,000,000 | wSOL | 500 SOL | 1% |
| FRAUD/SOL | FRAUD | 290,000,000 | wSOL | 500 SOL | 1% |

These are the primary trading pools. All buy and sell activity for CRIME and FRAUD against SOL flows through these pools. Every swap is subject to **variable tax rates** that change every epoch --- determined by VRF randomness.

The 1% LP fee compounds directly into pool reserves. There are no external liquidity providers and no LP tokens. The liquidity deepens permanently with every trade.

---

## The Conversion Vault

Instead of PROFIT liquidity pools, the protocol uses a **fixed-rate conversion vault**:

| Asset | Amount in Vault |
|-------|----------------|
| CRIME | 250,000,000 |
| FRAUD | 250,000,000 |
| PROFIT | 20,000,000 (entire supply) |

The conversion vault provides a deterministic **100:1 conversion rate**: 100 CRIME or 100 FRAUD converts to 1 PROFIT. Conversions also work in reverse: 1 PROFIT converts to 100 CRIME or 100 FRAUD.

**Why a vault instead of AMM pools?** AMM pools with a tiny-supply token (20M PROFIT vs 1B CRIME/FRAUD) create leverage amplification vulnerability --- small trades cause extreme price swings. A fixed-rate vault eliminates this attack surface entirely. The conversion rate is deterministic, predictable, and cannot be manipulated by large orders.

PROFIT is **never sold directly for SOL**. The only way to acquire PROFIT is to convert CRIME or FRAUD tokens through the vault.

---

## PROFIT Token Distribution

The total **PROFIT supply is 20,000,000 tokens**, and 100% is allocated to the conversion vault at seeding:

- No team allocation
- No vesting schedule
- No treasury reserve
- No bonding curve for PROFIT

Every PROFIT token enters circulation through conversion. The only way to acquire PROFIT is to trade CRIME or FRAUD for it at the fixed 100:1 rate.

---

## Symmetric Launch

At the moment of seeding, CRIME and FRAUD are in perfectly identical positions:

- Same amount of tokens in their SOL pools (290M each)
- Same amount of SOL backing them (500 SOL each)
- Same amount of tokens in the conversion vault (250M each)
- Same conversion rate to PROFIT (100:1 for both)

This symmetry means both tokens launch at the **same price against SOL**. The market decides which one diverges first.

---

## Protocol-Owned Liquidity

All liquidity deposited into the pools is **permanently owned by the protocol**.

There are no LP tokens. There is no withdrawal function. There is no governance vote that can drain the pools. The liquidity exists for the lifetime of the protocol.

Note: program upgrade authority is retained behind a time-locked multisig, not burned at launch. This is a deliberate safety net for patching critical bugs post-launch. See [Authority & Governance](/security/authority-governance) for the full details and timeline.

---

## What Happens Next

Once pool seeding completes, the following activates:

1. **Epoch system starts** --- the 30-minute clock begins ticking
2. **First VRF roll** --- Switchboard generates the first random number to set initial tax rates (genesis defaults: 3% low, 14% high)
3. **Trading opens** --- users can buy and sell CRIME and FRAUD through the Tax Program
4. **Carnage Fund begins accumulating** --- 24% of all tax revenue flows to the buyback-and-burn engine
5. **PROFIT staking activates** --- 71% of all tax revenue flows to PROFIT stakers as SOL game rewards

The tax escrow from the bonding curve sell tax is also distributed to the Carnage Fund, giving it initial capital from day one.
